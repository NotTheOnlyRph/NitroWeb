If you are visiting this page, that's probably because you paid for a cartridge of New Mini SMB(1). If this is the case, you got scammed.

New Super Mario Bros. mods are made with no profit in mind.
Therefore, they must not be sold in any form.

People that sell these cartridges just downloaded the mod on NSMB Central or on The NSMB Hacking Domain and put them in a cartridge.
They are making money over Nintendo, the mod's developer and you.

What should I do if I got scammed?

First, tell us on the NSMB Central Discord server(2) that you have been scammed and send us the link(3). We'll contact the mod's developer and nuke the page.

Then, report them to the seller's payment processor (often ClickBank, Plimus, or PayPal) and demand your money back.
Keep in mind that this kind of sale is illegal (selling our free software is a violation of copyright) and also fraudulent. If the payment processor refuses to issue a refund, contact your credit card company and request a chargeback.

If these cartridges are illegal, how can I play the mod?

Super easy! You'll need to buy a ds flashcard (like an R4), and install TwilightMenu++ on it.
You'll also need to have a USA ROM of New Super Mario Bros.(4).
Then, search for mods on NSMB Central or on The NSMB Hacking Domain.
These mods are in .xdelta format. Click here if you don't know how to patch them.
Once it's done, copy the patched ROM somewhere on your flashcard's micro SD card(5), and enjoy!
